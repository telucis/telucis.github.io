Sombre Rock

Loosely inspired by traditional sea paintings, this composite consists of 20+ individual elements combined. The main rock arch was the biggest challenge, and was achieved using a mixture of blend modes, masks and adjacent layers.

I feel that the image is helped by the sense of scale imposed by the rock arch and the tiny boat in the foreground.
